## The numbers

- 20 dials/day, M–F → 100/week
- ~25–35 conversations per 100 dials
- ~3–5 booked discovery calls per 100 dials
- ~1 close per 100 dials at the start, improving to ~1 per 50 by month two
- Plan emotionally for 3–5 weeks from first dial to first dollar

## The actual goal

The cold call's only job is to book a discovery call. Not to qualify deeply, not to sell, not to price. Book the 15-minute call or send the demo. That's it.

## When the script feels stale

Listen back to your own recordings before changing anything. Twenty minutes of audio review beats two hours of script editing. Pick three good calls, three bad calls, three random — adjust one thing per day.

## When in doubt

Dial. The tool is not the work.
